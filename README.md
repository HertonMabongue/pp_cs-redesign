# Stellenbosch University – Computer Science Department Website Redesign

A complete redesign of the Stellenbosch University Computer Science Department website. This project reimagines the department’s digital presence with a strong focus on usability, structured research output, programme clarity, and long-term maintainability.

- Clean academic design
- Structured and scalable content architecture
- Student-focused usability
- Research visibility
- Performance and accessibility

## Project Vision

University department websites should not feel outdated, cluttered, or difficult to navigate.

This redesign aims to:

- Present the department as a globally competitive research institution
- Improve clarity of programme information
- Showcase academic staff and research output effectively
- Provide better access to student resources
- Deliver a modern, responsive, high-performance web experience

---

## Core Website Sections

The redesign includes the following major sections:

### 1. Department Overview

- Welcome message
- Vision & mission
- Department highlights

### 2. Academic Staff & Faculty Profiles

- Staff listing
- Research interests
- Publications
- Contact information

### 3. Research & Publications

- Research groups and labs
- Centres of excellence

### 4. Undergraduate & Postgraduate Programmes

- Programme overview pages
- Programme structure
- Career pathways

### 5. Course Catalogue

- Year-by-year module listings
- Module descriptions
- Credit information

### 6. News & Announcements

- Department news
- Press releases
- Achievements and awards

### 7. Events & Seminars

- Upcoming seminars
- Workshops
- Academic calendar integration

### 8. Student Resources

- FAQs
- Forms
- Useful links
- Administrative guidance

### 9. Contact & Location

- Department contact details
- Map integration
- Directions and office hours
